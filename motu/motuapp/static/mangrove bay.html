chidambaram

mangrove bay camp

Mangrove Bay Eco Camp Hotel is located within 5 km of Pichavaram Mangrove Forest and 85 km from Pondicherry airport. Yercaud is 176 km away from the accommodation. The nearest beach is 150 metres from this Chidambaram luxury tent. Additionally, a separate toilet and a shower are available. For sports, guests can enjoy snorkelling, canoeing and fisheries.
